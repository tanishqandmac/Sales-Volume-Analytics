FAQ Section

  • Q. I have just installed the app, how much time it will take to fetch the orders?
    A. Unless you have very large number of orders, it wont take more than 10 minutes. Usually the app around 1000 orders per minute.

  • Q. Can I see the order volume within a custom range?
    A. Yes absolutely, just navigate to custom range in the navbar, select the range and you are ready to go!

  • Q. Do I need to sync all the past orders everytime I open this app?
    A. No this is only a one time process

  • Q. What about the future orders?
    A. We designed this app in such a way that it will automatically update the table whenever any new order is placed.

  • Q. What information do you store about my customers?
    A. We only store 4 fields - SKU, Product Name, Quantity & Vendor Name - that our necessary for this app to work. We believe in complete transparency with our clients and therefore no sensitive/extra information is stored about the store owner or there customers.

  • Q. What happens to my data after I uninstall this app?
    A. The app will automatically delete all the data from our database after uninstallation.
